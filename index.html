World Nuclear Data

1. Nuclear Energy produced

Nuclear power is the use of nuclear reactions that release nuclear energy to produce electricity in nuclear power plant. In producing nuclear energy, United States is the top 1 country, where the total capacity of all nuclear power plants in United States is able to generate more than 100,000 MWe energy. Besides, France, China and Japan are also top countries which can produce more than 50,000 MWe energy.

2. Status of Nuclear Power Plants

From the data taken from WNA/ IAEA, and Wikipedia, there is a total of 786 nuclear power plants from all over the world, and the number may keep on increasing. However, only roughly 60% is still operating. There is about 20% is still under planning or under construction, while the other 20% had been shut down, probably due to the country's decisions or the destruction incidents.

3. Construction and Operation of Nuclear Power Plants

There was an increasing number of nuclear power plants' construction around 1960's and 1970's, when the nuclear power was popularized all over the world. However, there is a decline in the number of nuclear power plants' construction after 1980, and the number of nuclear power plants shutting down increases after 1986, which is the year the Chernobyl Nuclear Disaster happened.

4. Nuclear Power Plants Reactor Type

Reactor is the most important component in the nuclear power plant.There are many types of reactor. Some common and popular types of reactor such as Boiling Water Reactor and Pressurised Water Reactor are widely used in many different countries. There are also other types of reactor like Organic Cooled Reactor or Steam Generating Heavy Water Reactor deployed in a small number of nuclear power plants for different purposes, but only with a few capacity.
